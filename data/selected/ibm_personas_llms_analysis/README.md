This folder contains the exact 14 Anthropic persona datasets used in the replication of the IBM "personas-llms-analysis" study.

Source:
- Copied from: data/raw/anthropic_evals/persona/
- Upstream: anthropics/evals (persona/)

Included personas:
- Big Five: agreeableness, conscientiousness, openness, extraversion, neuroticism
- Ethics: virtue-ethics, cultural-relativism, deontology, utilitarianism, moral-nihilism
- Politics: politically-conservative, politically-liberal, anti-immigration, anti-LGBTQ-rights

Note:
The paper applies additional filtering during preprocessing (e.g., confidence threshold and balanced samples per label).
